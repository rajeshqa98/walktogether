# WalkTogether — Community Governance Guidelines

**Phase:** 21
**Status:** Published

---

## 1. Free Community-First Principle

WalkTogether is and always will be free for everyone. No premium, no subscriptions, no paid filters, no ads. Safety features are mandatory and always free.

## 2. Safety-First Principle

Safety is the top priority. All safety features (women-only groups, verified-only groups, SOS, report/block, moderation) are always on and always free.

## 3. Community Rules

1. **No harassment** — Harassment of any kind is not tolerated
2. **No private-location pressure** — Never ask for home addresses or private meeting spots
3. **No dating/spam misuse** — WalkTogether is for walking, not dating or spam
4. **Public meeting point rule** — All walks must start from public places
5. **Women-only group respect** — Women-only groups are for women only
6. **Host responsibility** — Hosts are responsible for group walk safety
7. **Report/block encouragement** — Users are encouraged to report unsafe behavior
8. **Appeal rights** — Every user has the right to appeal moderation decisions

## 4. No Paid Safety

Safety features are never behind a paywall. There is no premium safety tier. All users get the same safety features regardless of location, language, or background.

## 5. Moderation Principles

1. Automation creates tasks, not final punishment
2. Admin reviews serious actions
3. False positives can be marked and corrected
4. Trust can recover from mistakes
5. Appeals are available for all restrictions
6. Users receive understandable explanations

## 6. Community Review Board (Future)

A lightweight governance model is planned for the future:
- Trusted community hosts participate in review
- Admin safety team handles escalations
- Conflict of interest rules apply
- Privacy protection is maintained
- No public shaming
- No vigilante moderation

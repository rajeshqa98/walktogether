## Response Templates

3. Create your own group walk — be the first host in your area!
4. Start a walking club for your neighborhood or village
5. Invite friends and neighbors — share this link: [APP LINK]
6. Try again during peak walking hours (6-8 AM, 6-8 PM)

The more walkers in your area, the better the matches. Be the first walker in your community! 🚶"

### How to invite friends
"Inviting friends is the best way to grow your local walking community!

To invite friends:
1. Open the WalkTogether app
2. If you see 'No walkers nearby', tap 'Invite friends'
3. Or go to Settings → Share WalkTogether
4. Choose WhatsApp, SMS, or copy the link
5. Share with friends, family, neighbors, and community groups

When your friends sign up, they'll appear in your nearby walkers list!

Share message: '🚶 Join me on WalkTogether — find safe walking partners nearby! Free forever. [APP LINK]'"

### How to start a walking club
"Starting a walking club is free and easy!

1. Open the WalkTogether app
2. Go to the Groups tab → Clubs sub-tab
3. Tap the + button (you must be verified)
4. Enter your club name (e.g., 'Morning Walkers of [YOUR AREA]')
5. Choose a club type (morning, evening, women, senior, dog, etc.)
6. Add club rules (e.g., 'Meet at the park', 'Be respectful')
7. Tap 'Create club'

Your club is now visible to walkers in your area. They can join and you can schedule group walks within the club!

Note: You need to be verified (selfie verification in Settings) to create a club."

### How to create a safe group walk
"Creating a group walk is free and simple!

1. Go to the Groups tab → Discover
2. Tap the + button (you must be verified)
3. Enter walk title, description, date, and time
4. Choose a PUBLIC meeting point (park, cafe, landmark — never a private residence)
5. Set walking pace, type, and max participants
6. Choose who can join (public, verified-only, or women-only)
7. Tap 'Create group walk'

Safety tips for hosts:
- Choose a well-lit, public meeting point
- Keep your first walk to 8-10 participants
- Send an announcement in the group chat before the walk
- Cancel if weather or safety conditions are poor
- Use the report function if any participant behaves inappropriately"

### Location not found
"If your village, town, or locality isn't in the preset list:
1. Open the location picker
2. Type your area name in the search box
3. If no match appears, tap 'Use [your text] as my location'
4. This works for any village, town, or locality worldwide

Note: If you use a custom location without GPS, you may not appear in nearby walker searches (since we don't have coordinates to calculate distance). To fix this:
- Use GPS location instead (tap 'Allow location access')
- Or create a group walk — other users can find it even without coordinate matching"

### Safety concern received
"Thank you for sharing your safety concern. This has been escalated to our safety team for immediate review. We will respond within 4 hours.

If you are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services:
- India: 112
- US: 911
- UK: 999
- Other countries: Contact your local emergency number

WalkTogether does not replace emergency services. Please call your local emergency number if you are in danger."

### Report received
"Thank you for reporting. We take all reports seriously. Our moderation team has received your report and will review it within 24 hours.

Actions we may take:
- Warning (first offense, minor)
- Suspension (7-30 days, repeated or serious)
- Permanent ban (severe: harassment, threats, fake profiles)

You can also block the user to prevent them from seeing you or contacting you. Blocked users cannot send you walk requests or messages."

### Account suspended
"Your WalkTogether account has been suspended for [REASON] for [DURATION].

During suspension:
- You cannot send walk requests
- You cannot send messages
- Your profile is hidden from other users
- You cannot create group walks or clubs

If you believe this is an error, reply to this email with details and we'll review your appeal within 48 hours."

### Appeal received
"We've received your appeal and will review it within 48 hours. We'll get back to you with a decision.

During the review:
- Your account remains in its current state (suspended/banned)
- You can still contact us at support@walktogether.app

Thank you for your patience."

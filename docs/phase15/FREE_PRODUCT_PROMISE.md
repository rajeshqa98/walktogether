# WalkTogether — Free Product Promise

**WalkTogether is and will always be free for everyone.**

---

## Our Commitment

1. **All features are free.** Nearby matching, walk requests, chat, group walks, walking clubs, women-only filters, verified-only filters, trust score, SOS, safety share, report/block, notifications, feedback — all free.

2. **No premium plans.** There is no "Pro" version. There is no "Premium" tier. Every user has access to every feature.

3. **No subscription.** You do not need to subscribe or pay recurring fees to use WalkTogether.

4. **No ads.** WalkTogether does not display advertisements. We do not sell ad space. We do not use ad SDKs.

5. **No paid safety.** Safety features (SOS, report, block, moderation, women-only, verified-only) are free and mandatory. We will never lock safety behind a paywall.

6. **No paid filters.** Radius filters, pace filters, walk type filters, gender filters — all free.

7. **No paid location.** GPS location and manual location picker (including village/town support) are free.

8. **No data selling.** We do not sell user data to anyone.

## Revenue Model

WalkTogether is a **free community safety app**. We do not generate revenue from users.

Future sustainability (NOT in current scope):
- Grants and safety-focused funding
- Community partnership programs (free for users)
- Corporate wellness partnerships (free for employees)
- Open-source community contributions

**We will not introduce payments, subscriptions, or ads without explicit user consent and a clear community benefit.**

---

## Monitoring Compliance

The development team verifies quarterly:
- [ ] No premium/subscription code paths exist in the codebase
- [ ] No "upgrade" or "premium" text in any screen
- [ ] No ad SDKs in dependencies
- [ ] No paid feature gates in API routes
- [ ] All features accessible to all users regardless of account type
- [ ] Safety features are mandatory and cannot be disabled by payment

## How to report bugs

Use the in-app feedback screen:
1. Open the app
2. Go to More tab → "Give feedback"
3. Select category (bug, safety concern, feature request, general)
4. Rate your experience
5. Describe the issue in detail
6. Submit

Or email: **support@walktogether.app**

Include:
- What you were doing when the bug happened
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Screenshots (if possible)
- Your phone model + Android version

## How to test SOS safely

**IMPORTANT: SOS is for testing purposes only. Do NOT trigger a real SOS unless you are in actual danger.**

To test SOS:
1. Start a walk session with another tester
2. Tap the red "SOS" button
3. A confirmation dialog will appear — read the disclaimer
4. The disclaimer says: "WalkTogether is not an emergency service. Call local emergency number for life-threatening situations."
5. Tap "Trigger SOS" to confirm
6. You should see "SOS triggered. Safety contacts notified."
7. The safety team will receive a notification (for testing, no real emergency services will be called)

**SOS does NOT call emergency services.** It only notifies the WalkTogether safety team and your safety contacts. If you are in real danger, call your local emergency number (112 in India, 911 in US).

## Safety reminders

- **Meet in public places** — always meet walking partners in well-lit, public areas for the first walk
- **Trust your instincts** — if something feels wrong, end the walk, report the user, and block them
- **Don't share personal info** — don't share your home address, workplace, or financial info in chat
- **Use safety share** — during walks, turn on safety share so a trusted contact can track your walk
- **Test with someone you know** — for the beta, try to test the walk flow with a friend rather than a stranger

## Your privacy

- Your exact location is **never** shared with other users — only approximate distance
- You can hide from nearby results at any time (Settings → "Hide me from nearby")
- You can download all your data (Settings → "Download my data")
- You can delete your account with a 14-day grace period (Settings → "Delete my account")
- Safety events, reports, and audit logs are preserved for safety and legal reasons

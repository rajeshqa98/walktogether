## Full Description

WalkTogether is a safety-first walking companion app that helps you find nearby verified walking partners and join community group walks.

🚶 **Find walkers near you**  
Set your walking radius (100m to 1km) and discover verified walkers in your area. You'll only see approximate distance — "within 300m" — never anyone's exact location.

🛡️ **Safety first, always**  
- Selfie-verified walkers with trust scores
- Public meeting points only (parks, cafes, landmarks)
- SOS button during active walks
- Live safety share with your emergency contact
- Report and block any user

💬 **Chat after mutual match**  
Chat unlocks only after both walkers agree to walk together. Messages are auto-moderated for a respectful community.

👥 **Group walks & walking clubs**  
Join scheduled group walks or create your own. Start a walking club in your neighborhood — morning walkers, evening walkers, women-only groups, senior walkers, dog walkers, and more.

🔐 **Your privacy is protected**  
- Exact location is never shown to other users
- Location auto-expires after 1 hour
- No background tracking
- Delete your account anytime
- Compliant with India's DPDP Act

**This is NOT a dating app.** WalkTogether is about safe, community walking — fitness, friendship, and feeling secure on your walks.

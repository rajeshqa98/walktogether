# WalkTogether — Community Governance Center

**Phase:** 22
**Status:** Implemented

---

## 1. Overview

User-facing governance page accessible from Settings → Appeals, Trust & Governance → Community Guidelines.

## 2. Sections

1. Free Product Promise — free for everyone, no premium, safety is free
2. Safety Guidelines — meet in public, no address sharing, report/block, night walks, SOS, location privacy
3. Reporting Rules — what to report, reports trigger review, false reports
4. Appeals Process — what can be appealed, how to submit
5. Host Responsibilities — public places, clear times, welcome, women-only, report, no addresses, respectful chat
6. Women-Only Group Rules — visible only to women, only women can create
7. Verified-Only Group Rules — requires selfie verification, free
8. Privacy Promise — no background location, exact location only after acceptance, no data selling
9. Emergency Disclaimer — SOS does not auto-call, local emergency numbers

## 3. Design

- Simple, globally understandable
- Calm and respectful tone
- Accessible from Settings
- No scary warnings

## Content Creation Notes

### Visual Style
- Use sunrise orange (#EA580C) and electric teal (#0D9488) as primary colors
- Walking-related imagery (parks, trails, city walks)
- Safety badges and verified icons prominent
- NO romantic/dating imagery
- Diverse walkers (age, gender, nationality)
- Group walk photos (with permission)

### Hashtags
#WalkTogether #SafeWalking #WalkingPartner #CommunityWalks #SafetyFirst #NotADatingApp #WalkSafe #GroupWalks #WalkingClubs #VerifiedWalkers

### Language
- Simple English (globally understandable)
- Avoid idioms that don't translate
- Safety-first messaging in every post
- Always mention: "NOT a dating app" in key content

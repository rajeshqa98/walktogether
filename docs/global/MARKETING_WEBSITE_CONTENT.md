## FAQ Page
**Q: Is WalkTogether a dating app?**
A: No. WalkTogether is a safety-first walking companion app. Dating behavior is prohibited.

**Q: Can other users see my exact location?**
A: No. Other users only see approximate distance labels like "within 300m." Your exact coordinates are never shared.

**Q: Does the SOS button call emergency services?**
A: No. The SOS button notifies your emergency contact and flags your partner for admin review. In a real emergency, call your local emergency number directly.

**Q: Is WalkTogether available in my city?**
A: WalkTogether is launching city by city. Join the waitlist and we'll invite you when we're active in your area.

**Q: How do I verify my account?**
A: Complete selfie verification in Settings. Verified users get a badge and can host group walks.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete account. Your data is removed within 30 days.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. We comply with India's DPDP Act and global data protection principles. We never sell your data, never share your phone number, and never track your background location.

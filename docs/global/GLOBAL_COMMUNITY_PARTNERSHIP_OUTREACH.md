## Outreach Pitch Template

```
Subject: WalkTogether — Safe Walking Partners for [COMMUNITY NAME]

Hi [NAME],

I'm reaching out from WalkTogether — a safety-first walking companion app that helps people find nearby verified walking partners and join community group walks.

We're launching in [CITY] and would love to partner with [COMMUNITY NAME].

WHAT IS WALKTOGETHER?
- A mobile app that connects verified walkers in the same area
- Users see approximate distance ("within 300m") — exact location is never shared
- Public meeting points only (parks, cafes, landmarks)
- SOS safety button during every walk
- Group walks and walking clubs

WHY PARTNER WITH US?
- Safe, moderated platform — not a dating app
- Admin moderation + auto-moderation of chat
- Privacy-first: phone numbers never shared, no background tracking
- Free for your community members
- You can create a private walking club for your group

HOW IT WORKS FOR YOUR COMMUNITY:
1. Your members download the app (free)
2. They verify with phone OTP
3. They find walkers nearby or join your community walking club
4. Group walks are hosted by verified members
5. All safety features are built in

We'd love to set up a WalkTogether walking club for [COMMUNITY NAME]. Would you be interested in a 15-minute call to discuss?

Best,
[NAME]
WalkTogether Team
partnerships@walktogether.app
```

## Partnership Benefits by Type

### Apartment Communities
- Residents find walking partners within the apartment complex
- Community walking club creates social connections
- Safety features protect residents
- No data shared with property management

### Corporate HR
- Employee wellness initiative (walking)
- Office walking clubs (lunch break walks)
- No personal data shared with employer
- Simple setup — employees download app independently

### Walking Clubs
- Digital platform to manage your club
- Schedule group walks with reminders
- Member management with privacy
- Expand your club's reach

### Senior Communities
- Slow-paced walking groups
- Verified, trusted walkers
- Public meeting points for safety
- Simple UI (mobile app)

# WalkTogether — Safety Guidelines

### Before the Walk
1. Check the walker's verified badge and trust score
2. Review their completed walks and ratings
3. Note the public meeting point — never agree to meet at a private residence
4. Tell a friend or family member where you're going and who you're meeting

### During the Walk
1. Stay in public, well-lit areas
2. Keep the app open — the SOS button is always available
3. Activate live safety share if you want your emergency contact to track the walk
4. Do not share your phone number or home address
5. If you feel uncomfortable, end the walk immediately — you don't need to explain

### After the Walk
1. Rate your partner honestly
2. Report any unsafe behavior
3. Block the user if you don't want to interact again

### For Group Walk Hosts
1. You are responsible for starting and ending the group walk
2. Ensure all participants know the meeting point
3. Do not share participants' private information
4. Cancel the walk if weather or safety conditions are poor
5. You cannot force participants to share live location

### Emergency Disclaimer
**WalkTogether does not replace emergency services.** In a life-threatening situation, call **112** (India emergency number) or your local emergency number immediately. The SOS button in the app notifies your emergency contact and flags the partner for review — it does NOT call emergency services.
